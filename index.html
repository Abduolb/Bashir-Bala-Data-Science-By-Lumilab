Vision

We envision a future where underserved youth are empowered with data skills to drive sustainable development and meaningful change in their communities.

Mission

Our mission is to bridge the digital divide in data science education by providing accessible, localized, and high-quality data science training to underserved communities, equipping them with the skills for 21st-century jobs.

About

The Bashir Bala Data Science Fellowship is more than a program—it is a living legacy. Named in honor of Bashir Bala, a visionary leader dedicated to empowering young people, the fellowship equips professionals with the tools to thrive in a data-driven world. Through a unique blend of technical mastery, hands-on mentorship, and community engagement, we create an experience that transforms theoretical knowledge into actionable leadership. Our fellows don’t just learn—they lead.

Objectives of the fellowship

To build robust pipeline of local talent with practical data skills.

To bridge the digital divide in data science education and employment access.

To create a knowledge-sharing community of data enthusiasts.

To foster innovation through region-specific data insights and problem solving.

To promote ethical data use within advocacy, policy, and entrepreneurship.

Program Components

Foundations of Data Science: Programming in Python, data wrangling, and basic statistics.

Applied Machine Learning: Techniques in supervised and unsupervised learning on regional datasets.

Capstone Projects: Fellows work in groups to address problems in different industries utilizing real datasets.

Open Data & Ethics: Training on using open data kits, licensing, and privacy laws globally.

Career acceleration track: Resume reviews, interview prep, and soft skills training.
